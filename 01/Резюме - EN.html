I create packages of edits for layout designers, if the site needs it. I know from experience that many factors influence the result, so my task is pull up all available factors.

Over time, I began to notice that not all Google businesses give the desired result, sometimes it happens for various reasons, but sometimes the problem is in Google advertising itself as a traffic channel. In this case, I I offer clients targeted ads to check if the problem is with traffic from Google ads.

Every business I work with is individual. I don't have a one-size-fits-all recipe. So I I work on the principle of trial and analysis. With some businesses, you can immediately find the right working principle. With some, I will say as it is, it does not always work out.
